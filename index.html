How AudioQR v2.2 works

Modulation: 8-FSK in the 1200 to 1900 Hz audible band. Each 55 ms symbol carries 3 bits, with a 10 ms guard. A 500 ms chirp marks the start.

Frame: chirp, training tones 0-7, 0xAA 0x55 0xC3 marker, length, payload, CRC-16, 0xC3 0x55 0xAA closer. Sent twice with byte-level majority vote.

Error tolerance: training preamble for per-channel gain calibration. Chirp threshold lowered to 0.08 with CRC validation downstream. Signal amplitude normalized to peak 0.95 for maximum SNR at the listener.

Short tokens: 3 to 8 character Crockford Base32 (excludes I, L, O, U). Stored persistently in your Cloudflare KV registry. The listener fetches the destination URL on demand.

Privacy: the listener page must be foregrounded and the user must tap Start to begin recording. No background or continuous listening. No data leaves the device except the token lookup itself.
